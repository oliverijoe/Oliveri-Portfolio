The man, Joe. The city, Dayton, OH.

My life has put me on many different paths - but none have been so formative than my time in Dayton. Blink and you'll miss it, this community has seen the greatest inventors and the most challenging times in our culture. I use it as inspiration to not simple 'make' but 'CREATE.' To not stop at what is possible, but to try, fail, try again, and succeed - for everyone.

My goal is take what I have been given and give it back in use for something good. Your dreams and ambitions excite me and I can't wait to get involved, to listen, and to create with you! There is no task too simple nor project that we can't work through. Give me a message whenever you are ready to build your website, your dream, our future.
